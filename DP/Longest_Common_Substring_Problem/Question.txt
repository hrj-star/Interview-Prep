

Longest Common Substring problem is the problem of finding the longest string(or strings) that is a substring (or are substrings) of 2 strings .

Substring - occupy consecutive positions within original string.


Example 

------------------

X = ABABC

Y = BABCA

Output
----------------
Substrings are : ABC,A,AB,B,BA,BC,C,BABC of which longest substring is BABC of length 4 .
